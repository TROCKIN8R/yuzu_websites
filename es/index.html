Transparent opportunity tracker

Refresh Watchdog

Monitor pipeline runtimes and alert the team when a refresh blows past its SLA. Inspired by a 78-minute refresh we cut to 4, the same pattern now watches Fabric semantic models, lakehouse jobs, and incremental loads.

In production, breaches route to Slack or Teams with pipeline name, runtime, and owner. Here we write every run to a live Supabase table and send an inbox alert when the SLA is exceeded.

  • Track: pipeline name, duration, and SLA threshold
  • Compare: flag runs that finish over the limit
