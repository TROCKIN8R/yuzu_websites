# Yuzu.solutions

> Si la vie te donne du yuzu, presse-le en tableau de bord. Conseil en données axé sur l'exécution — on construit le pressoir, pas des PowerPoints.

Yuzu.solutions est un cabinet boutique en données et BI fondé par Adrien Yvin, a Data Platform Architect and BI Leader based in Montreal, Canada. We help growing companies, enterprises, and post-merger teams turn raw data into governed warehouses, automated workflows, and boardroom-ready dashboards.

## Positionnement

- **Philosophie :** 80 % exécution technique, 20 % découverte — la stratégie est le sous-produit de la construction.
- **Modèle :** Partenariat direct avec un spécialiste senior. Pas de gestionnaires de compte, pas de délais gonflés.
- **Stack:** Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, SQL, DAX, Python, BigQuery, Snowflake, Azure, AI agents, Cursor.

## Services

- [AI & Automation](https://yuzu.solutions/services/ai-automation.html): Autonomous agents, API integrations, LLM wrappers, invoice processing, workflow automation. Stack: Python, OpenAI/Azure OpenAI, Power Automate, Fabric Notebooks.
- [Infrastructure & Data](https://yuzu.solutions/services/infrastructure-data.html): Medallion lakehouse architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold), ETL/ELT pipelines, Power BI semantic models, RLS, data quality gates. Stack: Microsoft Fabric, OneLake, Spark, Power BI.
- [Technical Debt & Rescue](https://yuzu.solutions/services/technical-debt.html): Legacy report recovery, DAX/model refactoring, refresh optimization (e.g. 42min → 4min), production hotfixes, post-migration reconciliation.
- [MVP Builds](https://yuzu.solutions/services/mvp-builds.html): Internal tools, pricing engines, customer portals, ML classifiers. Typical timeline: 6–8 weeks from discovery to production.

## Avec qui on travaille

- **Entreprises en croissance :** PME qui dépassent Excel, prêtes pour leur premier entrepôt et un reporting gouverné.
- **Enterprise consolidation:** Orgs unifying siloed systems, conflicting KPIs, and years of accumulated data.
- **Post-merger integration:** Teams needing one stack, one source of truth, and one reporting culture.

## Questions fréquentes

**What makes Yuzu.solutions different from traditional consultants?**
Traditional firms spend most engagement time on meetings and slide decks. Yuzu reallocates ~80% of time to hands-on engineering — writing code, building pipelines, and deploying agents.

**What technologies do you specialize in?**
Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, SQL, DAX, Python, scikit-learn, BigQuery, Snowflake, Tableau, ETL/ELT, Terraform/IaC, and AI agent development with Cursor and Copilot.

**Do you work with SMBs or only enterprises?**
Both. Common engagements include first-time data warehouses for growing SMBs, enterprise consolidation, and post-merger reporting unification.

**Where are you based?**
Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Remote-friendly across North America.
