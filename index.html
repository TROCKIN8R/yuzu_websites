🍋 Our Vision

Extracting Value

Raw data is just a crate of lemons. Our vision is simple — extract what matters, refine it until it's clear, and serve it in a glass you actually want to drink. That's ETL, the Yuzu way.

E Extract

Squeeze the lemons

We pull raw data from every source — databases, APIs, spreadsheets, that one crusty legacy system. Every lemon goes into the press. Nothing useful left behind.

→
T Transform

Filter, blend & sweeten

Pulp out, sugar in. We clean, filter, join and reshape the raw juice — fixing types, killing duplicates, and adding the business logic that makes it actually taste good.

→
L Load

Serve it in the perfect glass

The finished juice is poured into a polished glass — a governed warehouse and dashboards your team can drink from. Clear, fresh, and ready for decisions.

🍋
♻️ Always Fresh

ETL is refreshing. Automated, it's a fountain that never runs dry.

Squeezing by hand is fine once. But the real magic is a machine that does it for you — pipelines that refresh themselves on schedule, every morning, while you sleep. No manual squeezing. Just value on tap.
